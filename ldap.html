Ldap Enumeration

Enumeración LDAP sin credenciales 

ldapsearch -x -H ldap://<IP> -D '' -w '' -b "DC=<1_SUBDOMAIN>,DC=<TLD>"
ldapsearch -x -H ldap://10.10.10.182 -D '' -w '' -b "DC=cascade,DC=local"

Enumeración LDAP 

ldapsearch -x -H ldap://<ip> -D '<domain>\<username>' -w '<password>' -b 'DC=<domain>,DC=<TLD>'

Enumeración LDAP sin certificación 

LDAPTLS_REQCERT=never ldapsearch -x -ZZ -H ldap://10.10.11.202 -D 'sequel\sql_svc' -w 'REGGIE1234ronnie' -b 'DC=sequel,DC=htb'
